AOL Dial Up in 1990s

AOL started out in 1985 as a software company and eventually became the biggest name on the Web, signing up tens of millions of people for its dial-up Internet connection and popular e-mail service. Comedian Loni Love tries to replicate the screeching sound of a dial-up modem. "When we heard that little dial-up sound, that eeeeee, and then you connected, and you then go and you check your mail and you get that 'you got mail,' you were excited," Love says.
